LINUX检查开放端口

netstat -lnpt | grep 8118
netstat -nat|grep -i "443"
netstat -an|findstr 80 windows下
本地编辑：vi /etc/hosts
增加 127.0.0.1 ltb.cc
cloudflared tunnel --hostname ltb.cc https://ltb.cc:443 --origin-ca-pool /home/ltb.cc.pem --origin-server-name ltb.cc
ubuntu代理
export http_proxy='http://127.0.0.1:8118'
export https_proxy='https://127.0.0.1:8118'
export ftp_proxy='hhttp://127.0.0.1:10080''
export no_proxy='localhost,127.0.0.1
vi /etc/apt/apt.conf apt代理
vi ~/.bashrc 全局自启
取消ubuntu代理
unset http_proxy
unset https_proxy
